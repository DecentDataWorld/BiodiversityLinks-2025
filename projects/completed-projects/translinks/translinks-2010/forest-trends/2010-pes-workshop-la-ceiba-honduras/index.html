2010 PES Workshop (La Ceiba, Honduras)

August 9-13, 2010, La Ceiba, Honduras - The workshop, entitled Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystems Services (Taller Regional: Compensación y Pago por Servicios Ambientales), was hosted by Forest Trends, the Environmental Leadership andTraining Initiative (ELTI), EcoLogic Development Fund and the Rainforest Alliance, and was made possible by support from USAID through the TransLinks Cooperative Agreement.

Presentation - Basics of REDD +, national vs. sub-national approach, implications and alternatives

Presentation by Omar Samayoa, Rainforest Alliance, given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010, La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation reviews the basics of REDD+, the national vs. sub-national approach and the implications and alternatives available. Presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - Biocarbon, Facilitator of Enviornmental Services

Presentation by Phil Covell, Forest Trends, given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation reviews the role of biocarbon, presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - Commuity Forestry: Community Management of forests to climate change effects

Case study presentation given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation reviews community forestry in the community areas of the Gualaco and Guata national forest. Presentation in Spanish.

Presentation - Compensation and Payment for Ecosystem Services: Origin, Concepts and Structure

Presentation given by Beto Borges, Forest Trends, given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation reviews the origin, concepts and structure of compensation and payment for ecosystem services. Presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - Ecological and Social Principles for the Conservation of Biodiversity

Presentation by Javier Mateo-Vega, ELTI, Saskia Santamaria, given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation reviews the ecological and social principles for the conservation of biodiversity. Presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - Environmental Economic Incentives Program - ACP

Presentation by Arturo Cerezo, Environmental Economic Incentives Program (ACP) given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation outlines the Environmental Economic Incentives Program using examples such as the Panama Canal, the capacity and sustainability, the canal basin, programs and projects developed and environmental services programs. Presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - State of Forest Cover in Central America

Presentation by Omar Samayoa, Rainforest Alliance, given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation reviews the state of forest cover throughout Central America. Presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - The Role of Forests in Climate Change: Course Introduction to REDD

Presentation by Omar Samayoa, Rainforest Alliance, given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation provide an introduction to REDD and the role of forests in climate change. Presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - Avoided Deforestation project in the Multiple Use Zone of the Maya Biosphere Reserve (GUATECARBON)

Presentation by Associación de Comunidades Forestales de Petén (ACOFOP), given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation outlines the avoided deforestation project in the multiple use zone of the Maya Biosphere Reserve (GUATECARBON). Presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - Introduction to Environmental Services

Presentation by Rebecca Vonada, Forest Trends, given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation provides and introduction to environmental services, types of services, services provision, regulation services, support services, cultural services, and examples of environmental services. Presentation is given in Spanish.

Presentation - Experience of the Ipetí Embera Organization and Development Unit in the management of natural resources

Presentation by Organización de Unidad y Desarrollo de Ipetí (OUDCIE), given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation describes the experience of the Ipetí Embera Organization and Development Unit in the management of natural resources. Presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - Case Study of the Juma REDD Project

Presentation by Mariana Pavan, Idesam, given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation reviews the case study of the Juma REDD Project. Presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - Community Opportunities and Risks for PSA

Presentation by Erik Nielsen, University of Northern Arizona, given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in Le Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation reviews the community opportunities and risks for PSA specifically by addressing, the problem and dilemma, the role of indigenous people, the social impacts of certification projects and PSA, methodology of the community survey about REDD, preliminary results and thoughts on the design of PSA/REDD+. Presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - PSA and legal frameworks in Central America: Part 1

Presentation by Noelia Rodriguez, (ACICAFOC), given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation is the first part of a discussion on PSA and legal frameworks in Central America. Presentation is in Spanish.

Presentation - PSA and legal frameworks in Central America: Part 2

Presentation by Noelia Rodriguez, (ACICAFOC), given at the Regional Workshop on Compensation and Payments for Ecosystem Services, August 9-13, 2010 in La Ceiba, Honduras. Presentation is second part of discussion on PSA and legal frameworks in Central America. Presentation is in Spanish.
