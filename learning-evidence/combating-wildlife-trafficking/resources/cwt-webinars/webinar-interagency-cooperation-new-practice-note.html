Webinar Interagency Cooperation New Practice Note

The Global Wildlife Program (GWP)—funded by the Global Environment Facility and led by the World Bank Group—aims to promote wildlife conservation and prevent crime for sustainable and resilient development.

Published: 2023

In 2022, GWP published a Guidance Note on the Lessons Learned and Good Practices in Strengthening National Inter-Agency Coordination to Combat Wildlife Trafficking. In this webinar, Monica Zavagli, Counter Wildlife Trafficking Specialist, and Salvatore Amato, GWP Senior Law Enforcement Advisor, share findings from the report across five key themes necessary for effective inter-agency coordination: membership, governance, communication, resources, and effectiveness. These findings are valuable lessons for USAID-funded activities aimed at combating wildlife crime through an interagency cooperation strategic approach.
