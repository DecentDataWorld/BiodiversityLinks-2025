Library of USAID Situation Models and Results Chains

This library includes activity-level situation models and results chains for USAID's marine conservation and sustainable fisheries programs. They are a resource for understanding how different USAID programs are approaching similar issues.

The result chains are organized by six strategic approaches:

  1. Strengthening Decentralized Co-management
  2. Strengthening the Policy Framework
  3. Strengthening Organizational Capacity
  4. Strengthening Conservation Enterprises
  5. Building Constituencies
  6. Improving Science and Information Capacities
