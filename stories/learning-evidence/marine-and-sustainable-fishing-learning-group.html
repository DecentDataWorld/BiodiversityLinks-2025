Marine Conservation and Sustainable Fisheries https://www.biodiversitylinks.org/learning-evidence/marine-and-sustainable-fishing-learning-group https://www.biodiversitylinks.org/learning-evidence/marine-and-sustainable-fishing-learning-group/@@site-logo/biodiversity-links-logo-no-tag.png

This community of practice supports USAID’s programming for marine conservation and sustainable fisheries through peer-to-peer sharing and cross-mission learning.

Our current focus areas include:

  • Building forward in fisheries post-COVID,
  • Understanding and responding to the impact of distant water fishing fleets on national fishery resources and fisherfolk, and
  • Private sector engagement to strengthen fisheries management.

The community of practice is also developing a marine toolkit to support the implementation of common strategic approaches used in USAID’s marine conservation and sustainable fisheries programming.

If there are other issues you would like to explore through this community, please contact us.
